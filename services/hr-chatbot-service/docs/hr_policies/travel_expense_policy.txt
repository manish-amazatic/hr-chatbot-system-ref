# Travel and Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Company**: Amazatic Technologies
**Effective Date**: January 2025
**Version**: 2.5

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines guidelines for business travel, expense reimbursement, and ensures cost-effective, compliant, and comfortable travel for employees on company business.

## 2. Scope

**Applies to**: All business-related travel, accommodation, meals, and other expenses incurred by employees for company purposes.

**Not covered**: Personal travel, commuting to office, personal expenses.

## 3. Travel Approval

### 3.1 Approval Requirements
- **Local travel (same city)**: Manager approval
- **Domestic travel**: Manager + Travel desk approval
- **International travel**: Manager + Department head + Travel desk

### 3.2 Advance Notice
- **Domestic**: 7 days advance notice (preferred)
- **International**: 15 days advance notice (preferred)
- **Emergency travel**: Same-day approval possible with justification

### 3.3 Travel Request Process
1. Submit travel request on HRMS portal
2. Provide: Destination, dates, purpose, estimated cost
3. Approval workflow triggered
4. Once approved, book via company travel portal or travel desk

## 4. Air Travel

### 4.1 Domestic Flights
- **Fare class**:
  - Band 1-4: Economy
  - Band 5-7: Economy (Premium Economy for flights > 2 hours)
  - Band 8+: Business class (flights > 2 hours)
- **Booking**: At least 7 days in advance (for best rates)
- **Preferred airlines**: Indigo, Air India, Vistara, SpiceJet
- **Direct flights**: Preferred when cost difference is < 20%

### 4.2 International Flights
- **Fare class**:
  - Band 1-5: Economy
  - Band 6-7: Premium Economy or Business (for long-haul > 6 hours)
  - Band 8+: Business class
- **Stopovers**: Allowed if reduces cost significantly
- **Routing**: Most economical route within reason

### 4.3 Flight Booking
- **Booking via**: Company travel portal (cheaper rates negotiated) or travel desk
- **Personal bookings**: Reimbursement limited to company portal rates
- **Cancellation**: Inform travel desk immediately, change fees reimbursed if business reason

### 4.4 Upgrades and Perks
- **Personal upgrades**: Allowed at employee's cost
- **Airline loyalty points**: Employee can retain
- **Lounge access**: Via company credit card or employee's personal card

## 5. Rail Travel

### 5.1 Train Class
- **Band 1-4**: AC 3-tier or AC 2-tier
- **Band 5+**: AC 2-tier or AC 1-tier
- **Overnight travel (>6 hours)**: Higher class allowed for rest

### 5.2 Booking
- Book via IRCTC or company travel desk
- Tatkal charges reimbursed (with justification)

## 6. Road Travel

### 6.1 Taxi/Cab
- **For local travel**: Uber, Ola, or company-approved cabs
- **Bill submission**: Screenshot or invoice
- **Approval**: No pre-approval for < ₹1,000
- **Reimbursement**: Actual fare (within reason)

### 6.2 Car Rental
- For outstation travel or multiple local visits
- Book via company travel desk
- Sedan or SUV based on occupancy
- Fuel included in rental

### 6.3 Personal Vehicle
- **Reimbursement**: ₹10 per km (for car), ₹5 per km (for bike)
- **Requirements**: Valid DL, insurance, vehicle RC
- **Parking and tolls**: Reimbursed with receipts

### 6.4 Auto-Rickshaw
- For short local travel
- Reimbursed up to ₹500 per day without bills
- With bills for higher amounts

## 7. Accommodation

### 7.1 Hotel Limits (per night)
- **Tier 1 cities** (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.):
  - Band 1-4: ₹4,000
  - Band 5-7: ₹6,000
  - Band 8+: ₹10,000
  
- **Tier 2 cities**:
  - Band 1-4: ₹3,000
  - Band 5-7: ₹4,500
  - Band 8+: ₹7,500

- **Tier 3 cities**:
  - Band 1-4: ₹2,500
  - Band 5-7: ₹3,500
  - Band 8+: ₹5,000

**International**: Based on location, consult travel desk

### 7.2 Hotel Booking
- Book via company travel portal (preferred hotels with corporate rates)
- Personal bookings: Reimbursement limited to company rates
- Preferred: 3-star or 4-star hotels (clean, safe, convenient location)

### 7.3 Hotel Inclusions
- Room charges (single occupancy)
- Taxes
- Breakfast (if included)

### 7.4 Extended Stays
- For stays > 7 days: Service apartments or monthly hotel rates negotiated
- Lower per-night cost acceptable

## 8. Meals and Per Diem

### 8.1 Meal Reimbursement (while traveling)
- **Breakfast**: ₹300 (if not included in hotel)
- **Lunch**: ₹500
- **Dinner**: ₹700
- **Total per day**: ₹1,500

**Actual bills required for amounts > limits**

### 8.2 Per Diem (without bills)
- **Domestic**: ₹1,000 per day (for meals and incidentals)
- **International**: As per location (consult travel desk)
  - US/Europe: $75-100 per day
  - Southeast Asia: $40-60 per day
  - Middle East: $60-80 per day

### 8.3 Client Meals
- Business meals with clients/partners
- Reasonable expenses (no extravagant dining)
- Approval: Manager (for > ₹5,000 per meal)
- Bill must include names of attendees and purpose

### 8.4 Alcohol
- Alcohol not reimbursed (unless client entertainment and pre-approved)
- If included in group meal bill, separate out personal alcohol cost

## 9. Other Expenses

### 9.1 Internet and Communication
- Hotel WiFi (usually free)
- Mobile roaming (for international travel): Activate and submit bills
- Local SIM card: Reimbursed if needed for long stays (> 7 days)

### 9.2 Visa and Passport
- **Business visa**: 100% reimbursed
- **Passport**: Reimbursed if new or renewal for business travel
- **Visa photos, courier**: Reimbursed

### 9.3 Travel Insurance
- Provided by company for international travel
- Covers: Medical, trip cancellation, lost baggage

### 9.4 Baggage Fees
- 1 check-in bag reimbursed (if not included in ticket)
- Excess baggage: Only if carrying company materials (with approval)

### 9.5 Airport Lounge
- Via company credit card or reimbursed up to ₹1,500 per visit

### 9.6 Currency Exchange
- Use company forex card (for international travel)
- Or reimburse forex commission up to 2%

### 9.7 Laundry
- For trips > 5 days: Up to ₹500 per week

### 9.8 Covid-19 Related
- RT-PCR tests (if required for travel): Reimbursed
- Travel vaccinations: Reimbursed

## 10. Expense Submission

### 10.1 Submission Timeline
- Submit within 15 days of travel completion
- Late submissions: May not be reimbursed

### 10.2 Required Documentation
- Original bills and receipts
- Boarding passes (for flights)
- Hotel check-out bill
- Taxi/cab invoices
- Justification for expenses

### 10.3 Submission Process
1. Log into HRMS portal
2. Create expense report
3. Upload scanned receipts
4. Submit for approval
5. Manager reviews and approves
6. Finance processes payment

### 10.4 Reimbursement Timeline
- Processed within 10 working days of approval
- Credited to salary account

### 10.5 Advance Payments
- For high-cost travel (international, long trips)
- Request advance (up to 80% of estimated cost)
- Settle within 15 days of return (refund excess or claim shortfall)

## 11. Company Credit Card

### 11.1 Eligibility
- Band 6 and above
- Frequent business travelers
- Approval from department head

### 11.2 Usage
- Only for business expenses
- Submit expense report with bills
- Personal expenses prohibited (subject to disciplinary action)
- Monthly reconciliation

### 11.3 Credit Limit
- Band 6-7: ₹1,00,000
- Band 8+: ₹2,00,000

## 12. Group Travel

### 12.1 When Applicable
- Team offsites
- Conferences
- Client visits with multiple team members

### 12.2 Arrangements
- Travel desk coordinates
- Bulk booking discounts
- Shared transport where feasible
- Team meals allowance: ₹800 per person per meal

## 13. Conference and Training Travel

### 13.1 Conference Attendance
- Registration fee: Reimbursed (pre-approved)
- Travel and accommodation: As per travel policy
- Extend for tourism: Personal cost (only accommodation/meals)

### 13.2 Training Programs
- If company-sponsored: All costs covered
- If employee-requested: Partial reimbursement (50-100% based on relevance)

## 14. Client Site Visits

### 14.1 Extended Stays
- If client provides accommodation: No hotel cost
- Meals: As per per diem or client arrangement
- Local travel: Reimbursed

### 14.2 Client Hospitality
- Client dinners, gifts: Pre-approved budget
- Invoice must mention client name and purpose

## 15. Unutilized Tickets/Bookings

### 15.1 Cancellation
- If trip cancelled: Cancel bookings immediately
- Cancellation fees: Reimbursed if unavoidable
- Refund: Credited back to company

### 15.2 No-Show
- Employee responsible if failed to cancel
- May be deducted from reimbursement or salary

## 16. Lost or Missing Receipts

### 16.1 Policy
- Original receipts required
- Digital copies acceptable (photo or PDF)

### 16.2 Missing Receipt
- Affidavit or declaration (for > ₹1,000)
- Manager approval required
- Limited to 10% of total expenses per report

## 17. Prohibited Expenses

The following are NOT reimbursed:
- Personal entertainment (movies, spa, shopping)
- Alcohol (except pre-approved client entertainment)
- Traffic fines, parking violations
- Personal grooming (haircut, salon)
- Gifts for family/friends
- Damage charges (hotel, car)
- Extravagant expenses without justification
- Expenses without bills (except per diem limits)

## 18. Audit and Compliance

### 18.1 Random Audits
- Expense reports randomly audited
- Receipts verified
- Policy compliance checked

### 18.2 Violations
- First time: Warning and recovery
- Repeated: Disciplinary action, expense privileges revoked
- Fraud: Termination and legal action

## 19. Travel Safety

### 19.1 High-Risk Destinations
- Travel advisory checked
- Security briefing required
- Emergency contact 24×7
- Additional insurance

### 19.2 Travel Registry
- All international travel registered with HR
- Emergency contact provided
- Itinerary shared

### 19.3 Emergency Support
- 24×7 helpline for travelers
- Medical assistance
- Travel disruption support

## 20. Family Travel

### 20.1 When Allowed
- Long-term assignments (> 30 days)
- Relocation
- Approval from department head

### 20.2 Coverage
- Spouse and children (up to 2) tickets
- Family accommodation (larger room or apartment)
- Additional per diem for family meals
- Family travel insurance

## 21. Frequent Traveler Benefits

### 21.1 Airline Miles and Hotel Points
- Employee can retain for personal use
- Company doesn't claim

### 21.2 Travel Perks
- Access to premium airport services
- Priority check-in and boarding
- Lounge access via company card

## 22. Exceptions

### 22.1 When Exceptions Allowed
- Client requirements (client pays, higher standards)
- Emergency situations
- Health or accessibility needs
- High-cost locations (e.g., conferences)

### 22.2 Approval
- Pre-approval from department head or CFO
- Justification documented
