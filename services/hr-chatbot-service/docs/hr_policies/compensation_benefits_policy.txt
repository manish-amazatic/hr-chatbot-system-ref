# Compensation and Benefits Policy

**Company**: Amazatic Technologies
**Effective Date**: January 2025
**Version**: 2.5

## 1. Policy Overview

This document outlines the compensation philosophy, salary structure, benefits, and rewards at Amazatic Technologies, designed to attract, retain, and motivate top talent.

## 2. Compensation Philosophy

### 2.1 Guiding Principles
- **Market Competitive**: Target 60th-75th percentile of market
- **Performance-Based**: Reward high performers
- **Internal Equity**: Fair pay for similar roles
- **Transparency**: Clear salary bands and progression
- **Compliance**: Adhere to minimum wage and labor laws

### 2.2 Total Rewards Approach
- Base salary
- Variable pay (performance bonus)
- Long-term incentives (stock options)
- Benefits (insurance, retirement)
- Perks (wellness, learning)
- Recognition programs

## 3. Salary Structure

### 3.1 Salary Bands
- **Band 1**: Entry-level (0-2 years) - ₹4-8 LPA
- **Band 2**: Junior (2-4 years) - ₹8-15 LPA
- **Band 3**: Mid-level (4-7 years) - ₹15-25 LPA
- **Band 4**: Senior (7-10 years) - ₹25-40 LPA
- **Band 5**: Lead/Principal (10-15 years) - ₹40-65 LPA
- **Band 6**: Manager (varies) - ₹35-60 LPA
- **Band 7**: Senior Manager/Director - ₹60-100 LPA
- **Band 8**: Vice President - ₹100-150 LPA
- **Band 9**: C-Level - ₹150 LPA+

### 3.2 CTC Components
**Fixed Pay (70-80% of CTC)**
- Basic Salary (40-50%)
- House Rent Allowance (HRA) (40% of basic)
- Special Allowance (10-20%)
- Travel Allowance (optional)

**Variable Pay (20-30% of CTC)**
- Annual Performance Bonus (10-30%)
- Quarterly Bonus (for sales roles)

**Statutory Contributions**
- Employer PF Contribution (12% of basic)
- Gratuity
- Employee State Insurance (if applicable)

**Benefits (not part of CTC)**
- Health insurance
- Life insurance
- Accident insurance

### 3.3 Salary Progression
- **Annual increments**: 8-15% (based on performance)
- **Promotion increments**: 15-30%
- **Market adjustment**: As needed
- **Special increments**: Retention, critical skills

## 4. Performance-Based Pay

### 4.1 Annual Performance Bonus
- **Exceptional (E)**: 30% of annual fixed pay
- **Exceeds Expectations (EE)**: 20%
- **Meets Expectations (ME)**: 10%
- **Needs Improvement (NI)**: 0%
- **Unsatisfactory (U)**: 0%

**Payout timing**: April (based on previous year performance)

### 4.2 Quarterly Bonus (Sales Team)
- Based on achievement of quarterly targets
- Payout: Month after quarter end
- Formula: (Achievement % / 100) × Quarterly target amount

### 4.3 Spot Bonuses
- Exceptional project delivery
- Innovation/cost-saving ideas
- Going above and beyond
- Amount: ₹5,000 - ₹50,000

## 5. Long-Term Incentives

### 5.1 Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP)
- **Eligibility**: Band 4 and above, or high performers
- **Grant**: Annual grants based on performance and level
- **Vesting**: 4-year vesting (25% per year)
- **Cliff**: 1-year cliff (no vesting in first year)
- **Exercise**: Post-vesting, within 10 years

### 5.2 Retention Bonus
- **Eligibility**: Critical employees, key projects
- **Amount**: 20-50% of annual fixed pay
- **Condition**: Stay for 12-24 months
- **Payout**: At end of retention period

## 6. Health and Insurance Benefits

### 6.1 Health Insurance
- **Coverage**: ₹5,00,000 per family per year
- **Covered members**: Employee + spouse + 2 children + parents
- **Type**: Cashless hospitalization
- **Network**: 5,000+ hospitals
- **Pre-existing diseases**: Covered after 2 years
- **Premium**: 100% paid by company
- **Top-up**: Additional ₹5,00,000 available at employee cost

### 6.2 Accidental Insurance
- **Coverage**: ₹50,00,000
- **Coverage**: 24×7 worldwide
- **Premium**: Company paid

### 6.3 Term Life Insurance
- **Coverage**: 5× annual CTC
- **Premium**: Company paid
- **Nominee**: Employee designated

### 6.4 Parental Health Insurance
- **Coverage**: ₹3,00,000 per parent per year
- **Eligibility**: All employees
- **Age limit**: Up to 75 years
- **Premium**: 50% company, 50% employee

## 7. Retirement Benefits

### 7.1 Provident Fund (PF)
- Employee contribution: 12% of basic
- Employer contribution: 12% of basic
- Mandatory for all employees
- Withdrawal: Upon resignation/retirement

### 7.2 National Pension Scheme (NPS)
- Voluntary contribution by employee
- Employer co-contribution: 10% of employee contribution
- Tax benefits under 80CCD

### 7.3 Gratuity
- Eligibility: After 5 years of service
- Calculation: (Basic × 15) / 26 × Years of service
- Maximum: As per Payment of Gratuity Act
- Paid upon resignation/retirement

### 7.4 Superannuation
- For senior employees (Band 6+)
- Employer contribution: 5% of basic
- Post-retirement annuity

## 8. Leave Benefits

### 8.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Annual leave: 20 days
- Sick leave: 12 days
- Casual leave: 10 days
- See Leave Policy for details

### 8.2 Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: 26 weeks (fully paid)
- Paternity leave: 15 days (fully paid)
- Adoption leave: 12 weeks (fully paid)

### 8.3 Sabbatical Leave
- After 5 years of service
- Up to 6 months unpaid
- Position not guaranteed

## 9. Wellness and Work-Life Balance

### 9.1 Wellness Allowance
- ₹10,000 per year
- For gym membership, yoga, sports, mental health counseling
- Reimbursement on submission of receipts

### 9.2 Annual Health Checkup
- Free comprehensive health checkup
- At empanelled hospitals
- For employee and spouse
- Valued at ₹5,000

### 9.3 Mental Health Support
- Free counseling sessions (5 per year)
- Via partner therapists
- Confidential service
- Work-life balance workshops

### 9.4 Flexible Work Arrangements
- Work from home: 2 days per week
- Flexible hours (core hours: 10 AM - 4 PM)
- Compressed workweeks (with approval)

## 10. Learning and Development

### 10.1 Learning Budget
- **Band 1-3**: ₹25,000 per year
- **Band 4-6**: ₹50,000 per year
- **Band 7+**: ₹1,00,000 per year

**Eligible expenses**: Online courses, certifications, conferences, books

### 10.2 Conference Attendance
- Up to 2 conferences per year (work-related)
- Company covers: Registration, travel, accommodation
- Requires manager approval

### 10.3 Certification Support
- Company-sponsored certifications (AWS, PMP, etc.)
- 100% reimbursement upon successful completion
- Exam fees + training costs covered

## 11. Transportation and Meals

### 11.2 Meals
