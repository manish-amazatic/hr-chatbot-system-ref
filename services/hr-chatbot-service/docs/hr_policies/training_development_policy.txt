# Training and Development Policy

**Company**: Amazatic Technologies
**Effective Date**: January 2025
**Version**: 2.0

## 1. Policy Purpose

This policy outlines Amazatic Technologies' commitment to continuous learning, skill development, and career growth for all employees.

## 2. Learning Philosophy

### 2.1 Core Beliefs
- Learning is continuous, not an event
- Every employee is responsible for their growth
- Company supports and invests in employee development
- Skills development drives business success

### 2.2 Learning Goals
- Build technical and functional excellence
- Develop leadership capabilities
- Foster innovation and creativity
- Enhance productivity and efficiency
- Improve employee engagement and retention

## 3. Learning Budget

### 3.1 Annual Budget Per Employee
- **Band 1-3 (Entry to Mid-level)**: ₹25,000
- **Band 4-6 (Senior to Manager)**: ₹50,000
- **Band 7+ (Senior Manager+)**: ₹1,00,000

### 3.2 Team Budget
- Additional budget for team-wide training
- Managed by department heads
- For workshops, expert sessions, team offsite training

### 3.3 Budget Utilization
- Courses, certifications, conferences
- Books, online subscriptions
- Coaching and mentoring
- Professional memberships

## 4. Types of Training Programs

### 4.1 Onboarding Training
- **Duration**: 2 weeks
- **Content**: Company overview, culture, policies, tools, systems
- **Mandatory for**: All new joiners
- **Delivery**: In-person + online modules

### 4.2 Role-Specific Training
- Technical training for engineers
- Sales methodology for sales team
- Product knowledge for support team
- Domain-specific training
**Duration**: Varies (1 week to 3 months)

### 4.3 Leadership Development
- **Eligibility**: High-potential employees, managers
- **Content**: People management, decision-making, strategic thinking
- **Programs**:
  - First-Time Manager Program (2 days)
  - Advanced Leadership Program (1 week)
  - Executive Coaching (6 months)

### 4.4 Functional Skills Training
- Project management (PMP, Agile/Scrum)
- Data analysis (SQL, Excel, Power BI, Tableau)
- Communication skills
- Presentation skills
- Time management

### 4.5 Technical Certifications
- **Cloud**: AWS, Azure, GCP certifications
- **DevOps**: Kubernetes, Docker, Jenkins
- **Security**: CISSP, CEH, Security+
- **Data**: Data Science, ML certifications
**Company pays**: 100% fees if certified within 6 months

### 4.6 Soft Skills Training
- Communication and presentation
- Emotional intelligence
- Conflict resolution
- Negotiation skills
- Customer service excellence

## 5. Learning Formats

### 5.1 Instructor-Led Training (ILT)
- Classroom training with instructor
- Interactive sessions
- Group activities and discussions
- Typical duration: 1-5 days

### 5.2 Online Learning
- Self-paced courses (Udemy, Coursera, Pluralsight, LinkedIn Learning)
- Company subscriptions available
- Access 24×7
- Certification upon completion

### 5.3 Workshops and Bootcamps
- Intensive, hands-on training
- Duration: 1-5 days
- Expert facilitators
- Project-based learning

### 5.4 Conferences and Seminars
- Industry conferences (AWS re:Invent, Google I/O, etc.)
- 1-2 conferences per year per employee
- Company covers registration, travel, accommodation
- Share learnings with team (post-event presentation)

### 5.5 Mentoring and Coaching
- **Formal mentoring**: Assigned mentor for 6-12 months
- **Executive coaching**: For senior leaders (external coach)
- **Peer learning**: Learning circles, study groups
- **Reverse mentoring**: Junior employees mentor seniors on new tech

### 5.6 On-the-Job Training (OJT)
- Learning by doing
- Stretch assignments
- Cross-functional projects
- Job rotation (for high-potential employees)

## 6. Certification Support

### 6.1 Sponsored Certifications
- **Cloud**: AWS Solutions Architect, Azure Administrator
- **DevOps**: Kubernetes, Docker
- **Agile**: Scrum Master, Product Owner
- **Security**: Ethical Hacking, CISSP
- **Data Science**: TensorFlow, Machine Learning

### 6.2 Reimbursement Terms
- **100% reimbursement**: If cleared in first attempt within 6 months
- **50% reimbursement**: If cleared in second attempt
- **No reimbursement**: After 2 failed attempts or beyond 6 months

### 6.3 Certification Bonus
- Difficult certifications: ₹10,000 bonus (e.g., AWS Professional, CISSP)
- Standard certifications: ₹5,000 bonus
- Paid after successful completion

### 6.4 Exam Leave
- 2 days paid leave for exam preparation
- Flexible hours on exam day

## 7. Career Development Programs

### 7.1 Individual Development Plan (IDP)
- **Frequency**: Annually (during performance review)
- **Process**: Employee and manager jointly create IDP
- **Components**:
  - Career goals (1 year, 3 years)
  - Skills to develop
  - Learning activities
  - Success metrics
  - Review milestones

### 7.2 High-Potential (HiPo) Program
- **Identification**: Top 10% of employees
- **Benefits**:
  - Accelerated learning opportunities
  - Leadership coaching
  - Exposure to senior management
  - Stretch projects
  - Fast-track promotions

### 7.3 Succession Planning
- Identify successors for critical roles
- Develop readiness through training
- Job shadowing and mentoring
- Gradual transition

## 8. Internal Training Programs

### 8.1 Lunch and Learn Sessions
- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Duration**: 45 minutes
- **Topics**: Technology trends, project case studies, best practices
- **Presenters**: Internal SMEs, invited speakers

### 8.2 Tech Talks
- Deep-dive sessions on technical topics
- By senior engineers
- Open to all interested employees
- Recorded and shared

### 8.3 Knowledge Sharing Sessions
- Project retrospectives
- Lessons learned
- Best practices
- Brown bag sessions

### 8.4 Internal Certifications
- Company-specific product/technology training
- Mandatory for specific roles
- Badge/certificate upon completion

## 9. External Learning Opportunities

### 9.1 Conference Attendance
- **Allowance**: 1-2 conferences per year
- **Budget**: Registration + travel + accommodation (economy)
- **Process**: Submit request 2 months in advance
- **Post-event**: Share learnings with team

### 9.2 Online Courses
- Company subscriptions: LinkedIn Learning, Udemy Business, Pluralsight
- Individual courses: Reimbursement up to learning budget
- Free courses: Encouraged (Coursera, edX, YouTube)

### 9.3 University Programs
- **MBA/Executive MBA**: Company may sponsor 50% for high performers (2-year lock-in)
- **Specialized programs**: Case-by-case basis

### 9.4 Professional Memberships
- IEEE, ACM, PMI, etc.
- Reimbursed from learning budget
- Annual fees covered

## 10. Learning Time

### 10.1 Learning Hours
- **Dedicated learning time**: 5 hours per month during work hours
- Can be used for online courses, reading, certifications
- Manager approval for scheduling

### 10.2 Learning Days
- Attend conferences, workshops
- Considered work time
- Travel time included

### 10.3 Self-Learning
- Encouraged outside work hours
- Optional weekend workshops
- Access to learning resources 24×7

## 11. New Technology Adoption

### 11.1 Innovation Lab
- Dedicated space for experimentation
- Access to latest tools and technologies
- 20% time for exploratory projects
- Innovation showcases quarterly

### 11.2 Hackathons
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Duration**: 24-48 hours
- **Prizes**: ₹50,000 (1st), ₹25,000 (2nd), ₹10,000 (3rd)
- **Implementation**: Winning ideas may get implemented

## 12. Training Effectiveness

### 12.1 Pre-Training Assessment
- Skills assessment before training
- Define learning objectives
- Set expectations

### 12.2 Post-Training Evaluation
- **Level 1**: Reaction (feedback form)
- **Level 2**: Learning (quiz/test)
- **Level 3**: Behavior (application on job)
- **Level 4**: Results (business impact)

### 12.3 Training ROI
- Track investment vs. outcomes
- Skills improvement
- Performance improvement
- Certification pass rates

## 13. Trainer and Facilitator Guidelines

### 13.1 Internal Trainers
- Subject matter experts
- Train-the-trainer certification
- Honorarium: ₹2,000 per training hour
- Recognition at annual awards

### 13.2 External Trainers
- Vetted by L&D team
- Quality standards enforced
- Participant feedback reviewed
- Re-engagement based on ratings

## 14. Learning Platforms and Tools

### 14.1 Learning Management System (LMS)
- Central platform for all learning
- Course catalog
- Progress tracking
- Certifications and badges

### 14.2 Subscriptions
- LinkedIn Learning (unlimited access)
- Udemy Business (5,000+ courses)
- Pluralsight (technology skills)
- O'Reilly (books and videos)

### 14.3 Library
- Physical books (1,000+ titles)
- Digital library (e-books, audiobooks)
- Borrowing: 2 books at a time, 30 days

## 15. Cross-Functional Development

### 15.1 Job Rotation
- Temporary assignment to different role/department
- Duration: 3-6 months
- For high-potential employees
- Expand skills and perspective

### 15.2 Shadowing
- Spend time with colleague in different role
- Learn about their work
- Duration: Few hours to few days

### 15.3 Stretch Assignments
- Projects outside comfort zone
- Build new skills
- Increase visibility

## 16. Performance Support

### 16.1 Just-in-Time Training
- Microlearning modules (5-10 minutes)
- On-demand access
- Mobile-friendly
- For immediate skill needs

### 16.2 Job Aids
- Quick reference guides
- Checklists
- Templates
- Available on company intranet

## 17. Compliance Training

### 17.1 Mandatory Training
- Information security awareness (annual)
- Anti-harassment training (annual)
- Code of conduct (annual)
- Data privacy (annual)

### 17.2 Role-Specific Compliance
- Finance: SOX, accounting standards
- Sales: Anti-bribery, contract law
- HR: Labor laws, discrimination

### 17.3 Tracking and Reporting
- 100% completion required
- Tracked in LMS
- Reminders sent
- Non-compliance escalated to management

## 18. Manager's Role in Development

### 18.1 Responsibilities
- Discuss career goals with team members
- Create Individual Development Plans
- Recommend training programs
- Provide on-the-job learning opportunities
- Give feedback and coaching
- Support learning time

### 18.2 Manager Training
- People management training (mandatory)
- Coaching skills
- Feedback and appraisal
- Conflict resolution

## 19. Measurement and Reporting

### 19.1 Learning Metrics
- Training hours per employee (target: 40 hours/year)
- Learning budget utilization (target: 80%+)
- Certification completion rate
- Training effectiveness scores
- Skills improvement

### 19.2 Dashboards
- Real-time learning analytics
- Team-level reports
- Individual learning transcripts

## 20. Recognition for Learning

### 20.1 Learner of the Quarter
- Most courses completed
- Certifications achieved
- Knowledge sharing
**Prize**: ₹10,000 + certificate

### 20.2 Certification Wall of Fame
- Display of all certifications achieved
- Recognition at company events

## 21. Policy Governance

### 21.1 Ownership
- Learning & Development team manages policy
- CHRO approves
- Annual review and updates

### 21.2 Exceptions
- Beyond-budget requests: Department head + CHRO approval
- Expensive certifications: Case-by-case approval
